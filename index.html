Generate a number list with Ping Pong

Ping Pong will take a number from you and return a range of numbers from 1 to the chosen number with the following exceptions:


  1. Numbers divisible by 3 are replaced with "ping"
  2. Numbers divisible by 5 are replaced with "pong"
  3. Numbers divisible by 15 are replaced with "ping-pong"
